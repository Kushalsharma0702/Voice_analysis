Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the voice analysis?

VocalEdge AI uses advanced audio processing algorithms that provide a good estimate of your vocal characteristics. While it's not a substitute for professional coaching, it provides objective measurements and useful feedback that can help you improve your vocal delivery.

What audio formats are supported?

VocalEdge AI supports common audio formats including .mp3, .wav, .m4a, .aac, and .ogg files. For best results, we recommend using uncompressed formats like .wav when possible.

Is my audio data stored or shared?

Your privacy is important to us. Audio files are processed on our server temporarily and then deleted immediately after analysis. We do not permanently store your voice recordings or use them for training any AI models.

What should I record for the best analysis?

For the most accurate analysis, we recommend recording in a quiet environment with minimal background noise. Speak naturally as if you were in the actual situation (interview, presentation, etc.). A 20-30 second sample is usually sufficient for a good analysis.

How can I improve my confidence score?

Your confidence score is based on several factors including pitch variation, energy level, pauses, and filler words. To improve, focus on the specific suggestions provided in your analysis. Regular practice while monitoring these metrics can help you make measurable improvements in your vocal delivery.

Does VocalEdge AI work for languages other than English?

The voice analysis features (pitch, energy, pauses) work for any language. However, the speech-to-text transcription and use case detection are currently optimized for English. We plan to add support for additional languages in future updates.
